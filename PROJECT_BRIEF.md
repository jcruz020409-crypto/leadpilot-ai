# Project Brief

Status: draft

## Product

LeadPilot AI is a multi-agent sales autopilot for B2B prospecting teams and
agencies. It uses Qwen Cloud to analyze a prospect website, summarize the
business, detect problems, estimate deal potential, and generate a proposal.

Primary track: Agent Society.

Secondary track: Autopilot Agent.

## Problem

Software agencies and freelancers lose hours searching for clients, analyzing
companies, qualifying opportunities, estimating potential work, and drafting
credible proposals. The work is repetitive, but quality depends on consistent
research, clear assumptions, and a proposal that feels specific to the company.

## MVP Promise

Given a company URL, LeadPilot AI returns a Business Summary, Detected Problems,
Sales Opportunity Score, Estimated Budget, Proposal Draft, and Recommended Next
Steps inside a Final Analysis Report.

## Primary Users

- Software agencies looking for qualified prospects.
- Freelancers preparing commercial proposals faster.
- Founder-led B2B teams validating service opportunities.
- Consultants who need quick company analysis and next steps.

## MVP Scope

- Single URL intake form.
- Website content fetch with safety limits.
- Public social and sector research when sources are available and allowed.
- Business summary from public website content.
- Problem detection for AI automation, CRM, sales, marketing, operations, and
  customer support.
- Sales opportunity score with explicit reasons.
- Pricing estimate with complexity, ROI assumptions, and confidence.
- Proposal draft, outreach email, work plan, and recommended next steps
  generated by Qwen Cloud.
- Final Analysis Report that packages the complete multi-agent output.
- Memory record for analyzed companies, decisions, and user preferences.
- Result page with sections that can be copied or regenerated.
- Audit trail for AI runs and source URL metadata.

## Out Of Scope For MVP

- Fully autonomous sending without review.
- Bulk CSV lead import.
- Email sending and reply handling.
- Paid data enrichment jobs or scraping behind authentication.
- Native CRM writeback to Salesforce, HubSpot, or Pipedrive.
- Multichannel automation.
- Advanced deliverability warmup.
- Fine-tuning custom Qwen models.

## Success Metrics

- User can enter a valid company URL and receive a complete analysis in under
  90 seconds for a normal marketing site.
- Every result includes source URL, analysis timestamp, confidence, and key
  assumptions.
- Sales opportunity score and budget estimate are explainable, not black-box
  numbers.
- Proposal draft and next steps are specific enough to reference the detected
  business context and problems.
